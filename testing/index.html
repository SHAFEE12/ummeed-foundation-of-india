Who We Are

About the Foundation

Ummeed Foundation of India started with a simple idea: even small efforts can create real change when people work together.

We support students through educational guidance, career awareness, and mentorship so they can learn, grow, and achieve their goals.

Mission: Our mission is to ensure that every student, no matter their background, has the opportunity to learn, develop leadership, and prepare for the future.

Vision:A world where every young person has access to quality education and the confidence to shape their future.

Core Programs

Education Support

Career Guidance & Counseling

Public Speaking Training

Confidence-building sessions that improve communication, presentation, and leadership skills.

Skill Development Workshops

Hands-on sessions in digital skills, problem solving, teamwork, and career preparedness.
